FAQ

  • How To Mint A Fish?

    To mint our project, head over to our minting site and connect your phantom wallet to our site! Once you connect your phantom wallet, deposit enough funds for mint+gas. When the minting process goes live, the countdown will complete and reveal a "mint" button! Click and enjoy your fish friends!

  • What is ReefDAO

    ReefDAO is a future feature that will gain you access to exclusive voting rights for future project initiatives and plans. These voting rights will be exclusive to only owners of the Reef collection. Holders will maintain voting rights as long as they are owners.

  • Why You Should Hold Your Fish:

    We have designed a “self-sweeping” floor along with other programs to keep that floor price high. For example, if the price of fish begins to drop, people will be enticed to buy up that floor in order to FUSE fish at a cheaper price. As time goes on, there will be less NORMAL fish in circulation as a result of FUSION. Less of a supply -> higher prices on your fish! Combining this with ReefDAO, Reef Hangout, and a potential at 1 of 1s. That floor will stay sky high!

  • What is the Evil Scientist Lab?

    The pollution of humans into the reef has driven Simon The Scientist mad. In an effort to counter the effects of pollution, The Scientist has created a fusion chamber to combine fish together. By combining these fish, new exclusive traits are developed. (You must first visit link and click FUSE YOUR FISH. Then you will deposit 5 of your fish into the mission. These fish will be permanently fused into one ULTRA rare fish with exclusive traits never before seen.)

  • Is My Fish Unique

    Yes! Each fish is guaranteed to be 100% unique. The sharks have swam through the Reef to make sure that there are no imposter duplicated fish. They guarantee that no two fish are duplicates.

  • Is There Rarity Amongst The Fish

    Yes! Rare attributes will randomly be distributed to different fish. You will be able to view rarity via a rarity checker tool that will be implemented. Keep an eye out for these rare attributes as they can also appear on FUSED fish
