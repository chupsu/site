Lore

Welcome to The Reef

Home to over 3,333 unique aquatic creatures. Once upon a time, The Reef was a place of peace and calm, with beautifully tranquil waters of brightest blue, corals of every colour of the rainbow, and many different species, living together in relative harmony.

Irreversible changes

Unfortunately, the human world is not too far away from The Reef and if there’s one thing humans are good at, it’s pollution. A hundred years of chemicals in the water, waste-product dumping, and simple littering have changed The Reef and the Fish who live there into something very different!

Reef Explorers

Over time, the chemicals dumped into the sea have started to turn the Fish into a much more human-like bunch. Their intelligence has increased and they’ve started to make use of all the things the humans have dropped, wearing their clothes and accessories as status symbols.
Before too long, society was very different on The Reef! However, the growing levels of pollution threatened to do more than just change The Reef, it threatened to wipe them out altogether. Bringing his findings before the Reef Council, Muraenos Moray showed the gathered leaders of The Reef how rising pollution would affect them. A think tank funded by wealthy Crypto expert Balaen Blue put together a team of experts to investigate and explore the reef to find out more.
These experts are:

Muraenos Moray

Famed documentary filmmaker and ecologist, Muraenos has been raising information about pollution for over ten years

Doktor Von Skweel

The pollution of The Reef drove the dolphin mad. Mastermind behind The Laboratory, he plans to take over the world

Balaen whale

World renowned Crypto expert. With his wealth and genius brain, he plans to help defend The Reef

Carcha the Shark

Elite boxer and major investor into Balaen Blue’s startup, Carcha is also a climate activist.

Octavius the Octopus

Mistrustful of scientists, Octavious thinks that the pollution is a punishment from the Sea God and is along on the mission to keep an eye on the rest.

Yura the Crab Princess

Social Media star and reality TV darling, Yura is the princess of the undersea internet and keen to get #ReefReturn trending!

Bones the Prisoner

A fish turned skeleton, once was a minion of Doktor Von Skweel himself. After attempting to betray the dolphin, he was thrown into the dolphin’s dungeon.

Hip O’Campus the Seahorse

Muraenos’ sound specialist, Hip dabbles as a DJ on the side and is a big fan of Yuna. Looking forward to collaborating!

Major Chelonia Turtle

Keeping everyone safe is the job of Major Chelonia Turtle, a veteran of the Starfish War and decorated military officer.

Samael of the Deep

A lonely anglerfish who lives at the bottom of the ocean. Wants to help The Reef before the pollution reaches the abyss.

Army of Super Fish

Not all the members of The Reef agree on the best way forward. One, particularly sinister individual, a dolphin by the name of Doktor von Skweel, has found a way to fight back using human science. By deliberately using toxic waste and Evil Science, he has found a way to merge parts of five individuals to create one ‘Super Fish’, which he hopes to form into an army of Super Fish, first to conquer The Reef and after, THE WORLD! The Laboratory, long-held secret of Doktor von Skweel has just been discovered by Muraenos’ team. Who knows what they will find inside? Join us, and find out…

FAQ

  • How To Mint A Fish?

    To mint our project, head over to our minting site and connect your phantom wallet to our site! Once you connect your phantom wallet, deposit enough funds for mint+gas. When the minting process goes live, the countdown will complete and reveal a "mint" button! Click and enjoy your fish friends!

  • What is ReefDAO?

    ReefDAO is a future feature that will gain you access to exclusive voting rights for future project initiatives and plans. These voting rights will be exclusive to only owners of the Reef collection. Holders will maintain voting rights as long as they are owners.

  • Why You Should Hold Your Fish?

    We have designed a “self-sweeping” floor along with other programs to keep that floor price high. For example, if the price of fish begins to drop, people will be enticed to buy up that floor in order to FUSE fish at a cheaper price. As time goes on, there will be less NORMAL fish in circulation as a result of FUSION. Less of a supply -> higher prices on your fish! Combining this with ReefDAO, Reef Hangout, and a potential at 1 of 1s. That floor will stay sky high!

  • What is the Evil Scientist Lab?

    The pollution of humans into the reef has driven Simon The Scientist mad. In an effort to counter the effects of pollution, The Scientist has created a fusion chamber to combine fish together. By combining these fish, new exclusive traits are developed. (You must first visit link and click FUSE YOUR FISH. Then you will deposit 5 of your fish into the mission. These fish will be permanently fused into one ULTRA rare fish with exclusive traits never before seen.)

  • Is My Fish Unique?

    Yes! Each fish is guaranteed to be 100% unique. The sharks have swam through the Reef to make sure that there are no imposter duplicated fish. They guarantee that no two fish are duplicates.

  • Is There Rarity Amongst The Fish?

    Yes! Rare attributes will randomly be distributed to different fish. You will be able to view rarity via a rarity checker tool that will be implemented. Keep an eye out for these rare attributes as they can also appear on FUSED fish
