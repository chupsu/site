Story

Making a positive impact on the world on the largest possible scale was the motivating force behind the inception of the Peaceful Pelicans decentralized autonomous organization (DAO).


To serve this purpose, we sought to be an outstanding NFT project by filling in the lacking presence of NFTs in the tangible world with a project that leverages this new digital technology to preserve the survival of exotic birds.

Mission

Founded with the primary purpose of saving birds globally, Peaceful Pelican's goal is to protect pelicans using the new-age decentralized technology of the blockchain, financial.


Contributions to related foundations, and the promotion of healthy relationships towards endangered birds by inspiring environmental stewardship in general.
