Trees With Faces, TWF!, is a community-driven NFT collection of 9,999 programmatically generated freaking cool trees seeded and living as NFTs on the Ethereum Blockchain.

Each Tree With Face is a collectible that has been curated and randomly assembled from over 500k total options, each of them featuring shapes, outfits, faces and other peculiar elements that make each of them cool and rare on their own. Each TWF! is unique and no two are alike.

Entering the TWF! world, you don’t only hold an awesome PFP, but also join a family of cool friends in the Metaverse, with incentives like experiences, campaigns, $TREES token, liquidity pool, passive rewards, charity donation and much more. Are you ready?

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • WHAT IS A TREE WITH FACE?

    Trees With Faces TWF! are randomly generated, non-fungible tokens, as well known as NFT. There are a total of 9,999 different TWF! and they are minted, stored and traded on the Ethereum Blockchain. Each TWF! is completely unique. Generated from a smart contract using hand drawn traits such as heads, mouths, “hair” styles, eyes, clothes and other accessories such as cool glasses and icecreams, resulting in over 500,000 potential combinations at generation time.

  • HOW MANY TREES WITH FACES EXIST?

    9,999 Trees With Faces

  • ARE SOME TREES WITH FACES RARER THAN OTHERS?

    Every TWF! will feature unique traits/attributes, but some of them will be rarer than others due to the randomization of the generation of the NFTs at minting time. Once the collection is released, you will be able to see the traits for a Tree With Face under “Properties” on OpenSea, or, alternatively, ona third-party tool such as Rarity Tools. We will update further about it once the collection is released.

  • WHAT IS TREES FOR THE FUTURE?

    Trees for the Future (TREES) is a regenerative agroforestry nonprofit association that works with farming communities in semi-arid Sub-Saharan Africa to end hunger, poverty and deforestation by training farmers to regenerate their land. We commit ourselves to making our first donation to Trees for the Future after selling out the collection, and it will be the community who will take over the helm for any other future contribution.

  • HOW DO I PURCHASE A TREE WITH FACE?

    Pre-sale and Sale dates are To Be Announced both on our website and social media.

    Steps to Buy a TWF!:

    1. Download the Metamask.io extension on your browser.
    2. Purchase Ethereum ($ETH) from any exchange, such as Binance or Coinbase.
    3. Send Ethereum from this exchange to your MetaMask wallet.
    4. On launch day, open our Trees With Faces website and select the number of NFTs you wish to mint. Maximum of 10 per mint.
    5. Click the “MINT” button. A Metamask window will pop up asking for connection.
    6. Confirm the transaction and the associated fees.
    7. Once you have completed your purchase, your TWF! NFTs will appear in your wallet and on OpenSea.

    Note that once the collection is sold out, you can purchase a TWF! from our collection on the OpenSea marketplace.

  • WHERE CAN I SEE MY NFT AFTER MINTING?

    Once minted, connect to your OpenSea account using your MetaMask wallet to view your NFTs.
