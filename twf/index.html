Join the community

Trees With Faces, TWF!, is a community-driven NFT collection of 9,999 programmatically generated freaking cool trees seeded and living as NFTs on the Ethereum Blockchain.

Each Tree With Face is a collectible that has been curated and randomly assembled from over 500k total options, each of them featuring shapes, outfits, faces and other peculiar elements that make each of them cool and rare on their own. Each TWF! is unique and no two are alike.

Entering the TWF! world, you don’t only hold an awesome PFP, but also join a family of cool friends in the Metaverse, with incentives like experiences, campaigns, $TREES token, liquidity pool, passive rewards, charity donation and much more. Are you ready?

What about TWF! ?

You are about to dive into one of the coolest NFT projects to date, getting a fancy unique profile pic of your fav Tree With Face. But that’s not all, folks. TWF! is a community-driven project, you will be part of the future of TWF! : the community is the heart of the project.

TWF! OWNERS WILL HAVE ACCESS TO:

COMMUNITY VAULT

We are launching a Community Vault as the treasury to support our community. We will reserve 20% of all the minting funds raised to revert to the community in the form of giveaways, passive rewards, a comic series, and giving the chance to choose where the roadmap takes us to!

$TREES and LIQUIDITY POOL

We will provide an anti-paper hands system: Never sell below floor price thanks to our liquidity pool. Prices will be stabilised and even they may rise steadily in this way too.

Additionally, if you deposit your TWF! NFT into the pool, you will earn our token $TREES. Earn fees by providing liquidity, and rewards by staking your $TREES+ETH LP Tokens. More info will come will come soon.

Meet your Tree With Face on the TWF! Comic!

Your Tree With Face is not only a collectible stored as a ERC–721 token on the Ethereum network. It is also going to be part of the animated Comic series to be delivered on a monthly basis after minting. Each month, different TWF! characters with certain traits will appear.

Trees won’t be the only characters: in the future other characters will join the series, with potential crossovers from other collections.
