Data Scraping, Parsing, Crawling

Web scraping, parsing and crawling are all types of an automated process of extracting volumes of data from webpages. It is a must for marketing and SEO professionals to study the market, its main players, top competitors, offers, rates and prices. They use it both at the initial stage of running a business and while monitoring current changes. Since data scraping requires many requests made to a server from an IP address, the server may activate its anti-spam system and ban the IP. To avoid banning, use Cosmodata residential and mobile proxies which help to change your real IP address every time you scrape, parse or crawl. You won’t have issues with websites when tracking the info from news feeds, RSS and so on.
