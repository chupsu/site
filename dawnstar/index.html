Our Story

Founded by four angel investors and forward-leaning traders hailing from diverse backgrounds, Dawnstar Capital has been investing in digital assets and cutting edge blockchain projects since 2017 firstly as an angel investor group with consultancy services, and since 2021 under the Dawnstar Capital brand.

Dawnstar Capital is owner of CCC.io — a network of 20 channels and groups on Telegram and a Discord channel, and has a full-time team of professional developers, animation designers and marketers to cater teams with the utmost quality marketing and technical services.

Our Vision

We aim to build a place where you, willing to release a new project or running a consolidated blockchain project, can find not only funding, but also a committed team ready to deliver the best-in-class consulting, trading and marketing services to help you reach your goals.

Pioneers In Crypto

With long-term experience in trading and investing in crypto since early 2014, our team has been networking with most of the projects listed on Binance since its inception.

While building up trading and crypto news channels and communities on Telegram under the CCC.io brand, gathering over 500k active traders as of today, the Dawnstar Capital team has been fostering relationships and engagement with the founding teams of the projects we have worked with: interviews, marketing, and trading services.

Investments

Early-Stage Tokens

In the pursuit of broadening our services and being in the vanguard of the ever-changing blockchain industry, the Dawnstar Capital team is now devoting part of its resources upon early-stage tokens, investing in tokens during private stages at a discount off the listing price.

New-born projects do not only find investment in Dawnstar Capital, but also a broad range of marketing services and communities to help strengthen the organic growth of their token unti.

Liquid Tokens

Dawnstar Capital invests in liquid assets while providing technical trading expertise and in-depth crypto knowledge to teams.

Our team will review, invest and give advice for your project by providing the necessary tools to take your project one step ahead by assisting on development, marketing and trading. We are aware of the difficulty of developing while being up-to-date with the marketing needed to develop the project further to the next stage. Building a great product is not enough to succeed.

Marketing — Crypto Media

A sector as fast-evolving as crypto requires mingling not only the fact of being updated with the latest trends, but also reaching as big an audience as it is humanly possible.

Our network of channels and communities under the CCC.io brand positions Dawnstar Capital in an outstanding place to be at: get the audience your project needs through the forwarding of news from your project in real time and reviews of any novelties, so that the crypto community is aware of the latests developments from your project.
